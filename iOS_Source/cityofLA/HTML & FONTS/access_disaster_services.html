Get emergency notifications. NotifyLA is the City of Los Angeles’ official mass notification system used to send voice messages, text messages, and email messages during times of emergencies and disasters. Notifying the public when a disaster strikes might be the one and only safeguard the public can count on to save their lives and protect their property. It’s easy to get started. Text READY to 888-777 or fill in your information on the site at NotifyLA.
