Make your home safer to minimize injury and
damage during earthquakes

    • Secure and anchor bookcases and
      other furniture that may fall by
      fastening them to walls


    • Strap and brace your hot water
      heater to a wall


    • Remove heavy items like framed
      photos or decor from above your bed,
      or securely attach them to the wall so
      they will not fall on you during a quake


    • Place heavy and breakable items
      (bottled foods, glass or china) on
      lower shelves
